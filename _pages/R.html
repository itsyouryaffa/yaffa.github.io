Risk Analysis Project

✨ Project Description

This R project delves into the analysis of Euro Stoxx 50 data spanning from 1991 to 2022, aiming to provide deep insights into market dynamics and financial risk management. The project encompasses various analytical techniques and statistical models to evaluate and mitigate risks associated with investment in the Euro Stoxx 50 index.
